Every role is a watch. Whether you're on the bridge, at the armoury, or in the office — the standard is the same. This is your single source for standards, updates, and awareness across the Seagull Maritime Group.

The Framework

Assess. Report. Deliver.

Three actions that define how we work. Every task, every deployment, every day.

A

ASSESS

I assess the risks, the procedures and my task. I know the standard before I start.

  • Risk awareness
  • Know the procedure
  • Plan the task
  • Risk-based thinking
R

REPORT

I report what I see — hazards, near misses, concerns and ideas. My voice improves the system.

  • Speak up culture
  • Near miss reporting
  • Feedback & learning
  • Continual improvement
D

DELIVER

I deliver to the standard — every task, every time. My actions set the benchmark for others.

  • Operational excellence
  • Compliance leadership
  • Professional standards
  • Accountability
