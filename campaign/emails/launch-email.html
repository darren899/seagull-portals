Compliance Awareness Campaign

A new standard for how we assess risk, report concerns, and deliver safe operations.

What's launching

We're rolling out a company-wide awareness programme built around three principles. This isn't a tick-box exercise — it's how we raise the bar across every operation, every team, every day.

ASSESS

Know the risks before you start. Use BRAGs, risk assessments, and the screening tools to understand what you're walking into.
REPORT

See something, say something. Use the Safety App to report hazards, near-misses, and incidents — from anywhere, in seconds.
DELIVER

Do the job right. Follow the SOPs, use the documents, complete the training. Standards only work when everyone follows them.

Campaign Portal

Your one-stop hub for awareness content, procedures, risk guides, and compliance tools. Everything in one place — no hunting through folders or asking someone to send you a document.

Safety Reporting App

Report hazards, near-misses, and incidents directly from your phone or laptop. No forms to print, no emails to send — open the app, fill in the details, submit. Your report goes straight to the compliance team.

The app also gives you access to BRAGs and method statements through the Documents Library — so the guidance you need is always at hand, even when you're on a vessel with limited connectivity.
