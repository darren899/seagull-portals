# Seagull Maritime — Campaign Brand Guide
## "Your Watch. Our Standard."

### Fonts
- **Headings:** Montserrat (weights: 400, 500, 600, 700, 800)
- **Body:** Open Sans (weights: 300, 400, 500, 600, 700)
- Google Fonts import: `https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Montserrat:wght@300;400;500;600;700;800;900&family=Open+Sans:wght@300;400;500;600;700&display=swap`

### Campaign Slogan Treatment
- **On dark backgrounds:** YOUR WATCH. = white | OUR STANDARD. = #4DC4E8 (cyan-light)
- **On light backgrounds:** YOUR WATCH. = #003C72 (navy) | OUR STANDARD. = #00A3D9 (cyan)
- Both lines SAME font size (Montserrat ExtraBold/800)
- Always use company logo — no separate campaign logo

### Three Pillars
- **ASSESS** — colour: #003C72 (navy)
- **REPORT** — colour: #00A3D9 (cyan)
- **DELIVER** — colour: #42515A (slate)
