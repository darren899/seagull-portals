Due Diligence Portal
New client-facing DD procedure and self-service document portal — Live April 2026
We have launched a new Due Diligence and Client Vetting Procedure (SM/SEC/PRO/002) and a dedicated online document portal to support all client due diligence requirements. This replaces our previous process of manually compiling DD packs per request.
portals.seagullmaritimeltd.com
Password-protected • Unique access code per client • View-only documents
What Is The Portal?
A secure, password-protected online document library containing our full DD evidence pack. Documents are organised into 9 sections aligned to the OCIMF PMSC Guidance checklist and Shell audit requirements. Each client receives a unique access code and can browse, view and download documents at any time.
Pre-Completed Questionnaire
The portal includes our OCIMF PMSC Guidance Checklist pre-completed with approved Seagull Maritime responses across all 68 questions. Every answer cross-references the relevant evidence documents within the portal, so reviewers can verify our responses immediately.
Bespoke Questionnaires
If a client has their own DD questionnaire or specific requirements beyond the OCIMF standard, we can provide a bespoke completion within 72 hours. Speak to compliance directly to arrange this.
